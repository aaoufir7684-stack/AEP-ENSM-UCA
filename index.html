About Us

Founded on December 18, 2024, at the École Normale Supérieure of Marrakech, the AEP Club (Club des Amis de l’École Publique) is a dynamic student initiative dedicated to promoting the values of public education. Through social, cultural, and educational activities, the club empowers students to engage with their communities, develop leadership skills, and support inclusive learning environments.

What We Do

Our Activities

Engaging experiences that inspire growth and creativity

OCTOBER 02, 2025

The Emergency Response Workshop led By: Prof Laila Ouchen

The Emergency Response Workshop: React Quickly, Act Correctly focused on equipping participants with practical skills to respond effectively in urgent situations. Through interactive exercises and real-life scenarios, attendees learned how to assess emergencies, make quick decisions, and take appropriate actions to ensure safety and minimize risks.

November 08, 2025

Integration Day of AEP Members

We organized an integration day for cell members with the aim of promoting communication, sharing, and cohesion between members and their cell leaders. The day was marked by speeches, fun and interactive activities, moments of exchange, and a feedback session. This event strengthened team spirit and the bonds between participants in a friendly and dynamic atmosphere.

November12, 2025

The Green March Day

On the occasion of the commemoration of the glorious Green March, the Club of AEP took part in the celebration organized at the École Normale Supérieure on November 12, 2025. The day was marked by a variety of cultural and educational activities, including speeches, poetry, debates, artistic performances, and theater, all carried out in a spirit of patriotism, remembrance, and civic engagement. The event highlighted the role of youth in transmitting national values and preserving the historical heritage of the Kingdom.

November 19, 2025

Closing Ceremony of the 6th Edition of the Teacher of the Year Award

The closing ceremony of the 6th edition of the Teacher of the Year Award, organized by the Mohammed VI Foundation for the Promotion of Social Works in Education and Training, honored public primary school teachers for their innovative pedagogical initiatives. The event, attended by institutional partners and members of the Club of AEP, highlighted educational excellence, professional commitment, and the importance of partnerships in promoting the teaching profession.

DECEMBER 28,2025

Educational Actions Workshop led by Khalid Charri

The Club of AEP organized a workshop titled “Educational Actions”, led by Khalid Charri, aimed at enhancing the pedagogical skills of first-year education students. The interactive session provided practical insights into teaching practices, encouraged discussion and teamwork, and deepened participants’ understanding of educational concepts. This event highlights the club’s commitment to enriching student life and supporting the professional development of future teachers.

DECEMBER 29,2025

Personal Development Skills Workshop led By: Prof Laila Ouchen

This workshop focused on enhancing self-development skills. Participants engaged in activities such as chess, writing, and other exercises designed to improve critical thinking, creativity, and personal growth.

DECEMBER 12,2025

Atelier de la francophonie en musique co-créée avec l'IA

Dans le cadre du Jour des professeurs de français, et sous le thème « La francophonie en musique », l’atelier « La classe en spectacle : musique, théâtre et lecture co-créés avec l’IA », animé par M. Hossam Mohamed, a mis en valeur l’usage créatif de l’IA dans l’enseignement du français à travers la musique, le théâtre, la lecture et la poésie.
